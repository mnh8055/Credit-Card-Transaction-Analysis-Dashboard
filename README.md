# Credit Card Transaction Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes credit card transaction data to understand revenue performance, transaction behavior, card usage patterns, and customer acquisition costs. The Power BI dashboard provides interactive insights to support strategic decision-making in the banking and financial services domain.

## Questions (KPIs)

- What is the total revenue, transaction amount, and interest earned across all customers?
- How does revenue and transaction volume vary across different quarters?
- Which card category generates the highest revenue and interest?
- Which transaction method (Swipe, Chip, Online) contributes the most revenue?
- Which expense types drive the highest spending?
- Which customer job categories contribute the most revenue?
- How does education level impact customer spending behavior?
- What is the customer acquisition cost across different card categories?
- Which customer segments and card types are the most profitable?

## Process
- Collected and cleaned transaction-level credit card data  
- Created data models and calculated KPIs using DAX  
- Designed interactive Power BI dashboards with slicers and filters  
- Analyzed revenue trends across quarters, card categories, and customer segments  
- Identified key transaction and spending patterns

## Project Insights
- Q3 generated the highest revenue and transaction volume, indicating peak customer activity.
- Swipe transactions contribute the highest revenue, followed by chip-based transactions.
- Blue card category generates the highest revenue and interest earnings.
- Business and white-collar professionals are the top revenue-contributing customer segments.
- Bills, entertainment, and fuel are the highest revenue-generating expense types.
- Graduate-level customers contribute the most revenue among education groups.
- Customer acquisition cost is highest for Blue cards, reflecting a larger customer base.

## Dashboard Features
- Quarterly revenue and transaction trend analysis
- Revenue breakdown by card category and usage type
- Expense-based spending insights
- Revenue analysis by education level and profession
- Customer acquisition cost comparison across card types
- Interactive filters for quarter, gender, and card category

## Final Conclusion
This dashboard provides a detailed view of credit card transaction performance and customer behavior. By analyzing revenue drivers, transaction methods, expense categories, and acquisition costs, the project helps identify profitable card segments and customer groups. The insights can support business strategies related to customer acquisition, card offerings, and revenue optimization.
